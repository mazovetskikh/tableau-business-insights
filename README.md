## Key Business Insights

**Revenue Growth:**
Total revenue grew from $44K (Jun 2022) to a peak of $162K (Mar 2023) — nearly 4x growth over 9 months. December 2022 was the only month with a notable decline (-12.65% MoM), followed by a strong recovery in Q1 2023.

**Regional Performance:**
USA consistently drives the largest share of revenue across all quarters. APAC shows the strongest growth trajectory — particularly in Q1 months where it approaches EMEA levels. Total revenue by region: USA $643,880 | APAC $448,785 | EMEA $240,170.

**Product Revenue:**
Customer Success and Main App are nearly equal top earners ($44,670 vs $44,770), together accounting for the majority of total revenue. Publishing ($15,875) lags significantly — worth investigating whether it represents a scaling opportunity or a candidate for resource reallocation.

**ARPPU & Monetisation:**
ARPPU declined from $59 (Jun 2022) to $38 (May 2023) as the paid user base scaled from ~200 to ~3,500. This is a healthy signal — volume growth outpacing per-user revenue decline indicates successful market expansion rather than monetisation weakness.

**Cohort Revenue Retention:**
The June 2022 cohort generated the highest new MMR ($44,085) and shows the longest revenue tail — still producing $9K at month 11. Later cohorts are smaller but follow consistent patterns, suggesting stable acquisition quality over time.
